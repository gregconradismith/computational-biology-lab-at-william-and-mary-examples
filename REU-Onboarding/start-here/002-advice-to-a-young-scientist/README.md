## Note

Function as independently as possible, but take personal responsibility for getting the help you need.

Advising research students is one of the most enjoyable aspects of being a professor. You are always allowed to interrupt me from the less enjoyable aspects of my work. On the other hand, the lab operates most efficiently when questions are asked of the most junior lab member who might be expected to know the answer.

Treat research like a job. Be self-motivated, professional, and meet deadlines. Read the primary literature. Have your own ideas. Be results-oriented. Be curious. Be yourself.
